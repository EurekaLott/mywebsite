Forecast Rules

1. Choose ONE Side Only
Select either LEFT AI SIGNALS or RIGHT AI SIGNALS.
Do not combine both sides.


2. Latest Draw
The Latest Draw is the reference draw used to generate the AI Signals.
It is NOT the draw for using this forecast.


3. Three Checkpoints

Imagine there are three checkpoints on the journey toward the Final Destination.

  • Checkpoint 1: If either AI Signal on your chosen side appears, stop tracking this forecast immediately.
  • Checkpoint 2: If either AI Signal on your chosen side appears, stop tracking this forecast immediately.
  • Only if neither AI Signal appears at Checkpoint 1 or Checkpoint 2 should you continue to the Final Destination.

4. Final Destination

The third draw is the Final Destination.

At this point, choose ONE number from your selected AI Signal pair.

If your chosen number appears in this draw, the AI forecast is considered successful.


5. Help Improve EurekaLott

If you find that this forecasting method does not perform as expected, even a single time, please report it to us.

Every report is valuable in helping improve the system and removing methods that no longer meet EurekaLott's quality standards.
